# 📧 Email Visual Previews

## 🎨 Design System

### Color Palette

```
Primary:     #4F46E5 (Brand Blue)     ▮
Accent:      #10B981 (Success Green)  ▮
Warning:     #F59E0B (Amber)          ▮
Danger:      #EF4444 (Red)            ▮
Neutral 100: #F9FAFB (Light Gray)     ▮
Neutral 500: #6B7280 (Medium Gray)    ▮
Neutral 900: #111827 (Dark Text)      ▮
```

### Typography

- **Header Title:** 28px, Bold, Dark
- **Section Title:** 18px, Semi-bold
- **Body Text:** 14px, Regular
- **Small Text:** 12px, Regular (labels)
- **Font:** System fonts (Gmail/Outlook compatible)

### Layout

- **Width:** 640px max (mobile responsive)
- **Padding:** 24-28px
- **Border Radius:** 6-12px
- **Shadows:** Light (0 4px 6px rgba(0,0,0,0.1))

## 🎯 Key Design Elements

### Headers

- ✅ Gradient background (Brand to Purple)
- ✅ White text
- ✅ Clear hierarchy (Nido Tech → Title → Subtitle)
- ✅ 40px padding

### Content Cards

- ✅ Light gray background (#F9FAFB)
- ✅ Rounded corners (8-12px)
- ✅ Subtle borders
- ✅ Left border accent (4px)
- ✅ Proper spacing

### Data Rows

- ✅ Flexbox layout
- ✅ Label on left (gray)
- ✅ Value on right (dark)
- ✅ Bottom border for separation
- ✅ Balanced spacing

### Tables

- ✅ Full width
- ✅ Clear header row
- ✅ Left-align text
- ✅ Right-align numbers
- ✅ Subtle bottom borders
- ✅ Hover-friendly colors

### Buttons

- ✅ Brand color (#4F46E5)
- ✅ Inline padding (12px 28px)
- ✅ Rounded (6px)
- ✅ White text
- ✅ No underline
- ✅ Readable font size

### Badges

- ✅ Multiple variants (primary, success, warning, danger)
- ✅ Uppercase text
- ✅ Rounded (20px)
- ✅ Padding (6px 12px)
- ✅ Font weight 600

### Footers

- ✅ Light background
- ✅ Contact information
- ✅ Social links
- ✅ Copyright text
- ✅ 28px padding
- ✅ 12px font size
